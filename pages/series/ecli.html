Eclipse

Fading, the power of seeing imperfectly. What if everything I see turns into a memory, replacing only an aura of action and pieces of color with the sharpness of reality? The following collection represents an imaginary utopia. A utopia in which the pain of remembering is constantly diminished, events are reduced(or ascended) to the chaos of a few waves of light, and the passing of everything is so fast (and light) that the faulty conscious will no longer have authority.
